What can we do for you?

We cater

We have over 10 years of experience to cater for all type of events. Weddings, anniversaries, royal parties *wink wink*.
Call us or visit us for a free quote.

We workshop

We organize awesome theme-flower workshops all year round, both private and public. Check out our calendar to find out more or call us to book your private event.

We deliver

We don't use a parachute and we are currently unable to deliver outside the UK. We can deliver up to 3 hours before, within a certain mile radius. We are awesome but can't do miracles (yet).

Discover the benefits of herbal tea

Come and sit with us in our little garden.
We offer a small selection of herbal tea for you to enjoy hot in our small patio, or you can purchase and carry home with you.
